Connection
==========

> The `connection` object gives access to the device's cellular and wifi connection information.

This object is accessed under the navigator.network interface.

Properties
----------

- connection.type

Constants
---------

- Connection.ETHERNET
- Connection.WIFI
- Connection.CELL_2G
- Connection.CELL_3G
- Connection.CELL_4G
- Connection.NONE
